Tokens are sold to strategic partners to fund a solid runway for the company, without selling too large of a share, so as to allow for a larger reserve for contingencies.

This goal is further supported by applying longer vesting schedules to all sales. This structure ensures that FLUID only receives capital from strategic partners who are willing to go the distance to work with us and support the project.

The chosen vesting schedules also commit FLUID’s team to a long-term vision. Allocations funding internal departmental budgets have been given vesting terms that last 4 to 5 years.
